Welcome

Department of Computer Science

BCA stands for Bachelor of Computer Applications, and it is an undergraduate academic degree in the field of computer applications. This program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in key areas of computer science and applications. BCA typically covers subjects such as programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, database management systems, software engineering, and web development.
